About Me

I'm Former, a 16-year-old Minecraft developer and professional Discord staff member who enjoys listening to music and helping communities feel organized, welcoming, and fun to be part of. I started playing Minecraft in 2019 on Bedrock Edition (mobile) and moved to Java Edition in 2021. I began working on Minecraft servers in June, driven by a genuine interest in how multiplayer communities function and how small technical details can make a big difference in a player's experience.

Over time, I've gained experience setting up servers, configuring multiple plugins, and designing systems that keep gameplay smooth and fair. I also work closely with Discord staff teams and have set up multiple Discord servers, creating role structures, moderation guidelines, and workflows that make staffing efficient and effective.

What I enjoy most is turning ideas into something functional and reliable. Whether it's improving server performance, organizing staff roles and duties, or creating webhooks and system messages, I take pride in building systems that people can trust and enjoy using.
